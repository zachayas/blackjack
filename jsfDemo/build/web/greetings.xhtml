Welcome to 21 Blackjack!

To start playing just hit the play button below.

Rules

You can bet money from $5, $10, $15, and if you win you'll make double your amount

To win, either have a higher value than the dealer or hit 21. If you win with 21, then you'll earn 2.5 your amount.

If you are under the dealer or over 21 then you lose.

If you and the dealer tie or "push", then you don't win anything nor lose anything

If this is your first time, we'll give you a base of $50 to play. Your Money will be stored and shown below.
