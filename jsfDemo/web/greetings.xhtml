Welcome to 21 Blackjack!

To start playing just hit the play button below.

Rules

If you made the table, you're given $50.

To win, either have a higher value than the dealer or hit 21. If you win you will earn 2 your amount.

If you are under the dealer or over 21 then you lose half of your money.

If you and the dealer tie or "push", then your money is safe.

Your Money will be stored and shown below.

How to play

Press the Hit buttons to place a card on the table.

Once you are satisfied, press the Deal button to deal with the dealer.

A data table will show if you won or lost or push, the value, and the cards used.
